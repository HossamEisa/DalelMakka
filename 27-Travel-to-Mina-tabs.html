Mina is a small town situated at a distance of 5 or 6 kilometres from Makkah. Another name of the town is the ‘City of tents‘. After leaving Makkah, pilgrims spend their first day and night in the tents of this city. This is the second ritual of Hajj where pilgrims spend whole day and night offering the prayers (compulsory and non-compulsory). These tents are equipped with almost all modern amenities.
